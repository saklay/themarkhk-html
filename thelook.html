Sustainability

The Mark is committed to sustainability, supporting a growing generation of environmentally conscious companies and bringing broader benefits to both tenants and the wider community: These benefits include reduced energy and water costs, reduced waste, better air quality and a smaller carbon footprint for Hong Kong. Yet, The Mark goes beyond this by also applying for the BEAM Plus green building label. Developed with widespread industry engagement, BEAM Plus is Hong Kong's home-grown rating system to enhance building environmental sustainability. The system defines over 100 criteria that promote sustainable sites and healthy indoor environments whilst reducing energy, water and resources consumption and carbon footprint.

Architectural layout and building facilities

Features and facilities
- Covered car drop off and loading at street level inside the building.
- Close proximity to Kwun Tong and Ngau Tau Kok MTR Stations and Kwun Tong Ferry Pier.
- Ample car parking available for tenants.
- Free-to-tenants floor with a range of amenities and services for tenants.
Office lobbies
- Main office lobby at ground floor.
- Covered entrance with direct access from car park to office lobby.
Gross floor area
- From approx. 1,624 to 3,484 sq.ft. gross.
- Typical floor dimensions: approx. 10.8m/35.4 ft x 11.7m/38.4 ft (half-floor) to 24.8m/81.4 ft (full-floor)
Window wall
- Glazed, coated with high performance reflective and low-e coatings.
- Vertical mullions set at 1m to 2m intervals approx.
- Large glass panels allow maximum natural light penetration.
Floor loading
- Floor loadings are designed for imposed loads of 7.5kPa.
Ceiling height
- Slab-to-slab ceiling heights: G/F: 5.00m; 1/F: 3.10m; 2/F: 4.75m; 3/F-20/F: 3.00m approx.

Landlord's provision

False ceiling system (optional)
- Acoustic mineral wool ceiling in 600x600 mm panels.
- Lighting panels in 600x600 mm with T5 fluorescent tubes and electronic ballasts.
Electrical
- Separate electrical riser closets incorporating tenant utility power, fire services power, backup power and space for tenant dedicated risers.
Air-conditioning
- Unitary VRV air-conditioning system is provided for each tenancy unit.
- Fresh air fan is provided inside each tenancy unit.
- Condensate drain riser and VRV indoor units are provided.

Fire services
- Automatic sprinkler system.
- AFA and MFA systems.
- Emergency power and lighting, fire hydrant/hose reels are installed to comply with government requirements.
Plumbing and drainage
- Sanitary drainage and water supply systems are provided.
- Men's, women's and disabled washrooms on every office floor.
- All washrooms equipped with "no touch" sensor operated water taps; urinal and water closets.

Vertical transportation

Passenger and vehicular lifts
- L1 to L2 (all floors): Two passenger lifts each with 1,350 kg capacity/ 18 persons.
- L3 (G/F to 2/F car park): Vehicular lift with 3,500 kg capacity.

Air-conditioning and mechanical ventilation systems

The system
- Fresh air supply for tenancy unit at 10 litre/second/person.
- Fresh air fan is provided inside each tenancy unit.
- Nominal cooling capacities allowed for tenancy units are: 3/F: 50.4kW; 4/F to 20/F: Office A: 22.4kW, Office B: 28kW.
- Mechanical exhaust provided for all washrooms.
Unitary Air-conditioner
- All the VRV units use environmentally acceptable R410A refrigerant.
- VRV outdoor units for tenancy units are installed on the new AC platform on each floor.

Fire Services and Security Systems

Automatic sprinkler system
- The entire building is protected by an automatic sprinkler system which complies with BS EN 12845:2003. 80 cum. sprinkler water storage tanks are installed at underground level.
Fire hydrant and fire hose system
- Hydrant outlet located at each staircase landing and hose reels provided at public areas on each floor. The location will be determined such that all parts of the building can be reached by the 30m hose. 27 cum. FS water storage tanks are installed at upper roof level.
Automatic fire alarm system (AFA)
- The fire detection and alarm system consists of heat detectors, smoke detectors, break glass units, flow switches and visual/audio alarms.
- On activation of the AFA, fire alarm signals will be sent to the building's control room and directly to the Fire Services Department.
CCTV security system
- The building is monitored 24/7 by a CCTV System that covers all major access/exits points, lift cars and common areas.
- All cameras are connected to digital video recording system.
Intrusion detection
- The building is equipped with a door monitoring alarm system for all fire exit doors open to G/F outdoor and main roof exit doors with detex lock and magnetic door contact concealed in doorframe.
Uninterrupted power supply (UPS)
- The CCTV system is supported by a UPS unit that ensures control and security systems remain operational in the event of utilities power failure.
